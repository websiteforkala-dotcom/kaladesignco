Founding Partner

Amit Das

Amit is the Founding Partner of Kala Design Co. He studied architecture at the Faculty of Architecture & Ekistics, Jamia Millia Islamia. A heritage enthusiast and dedicated designer, Amit is committed to spaces that address the sacred relationship between man and environment, responding to local economics, sociology, and history. His approach is meditative, focusing on the unspoken dialogue between materials and light.

Principal Architect & Co-founder

Kanishk Mittal

Kanishk is the Principal Architect and Co-founder at Kala Design Co. An alumnus of Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur, he brings extensive experience in architectural design, project management, and interior design across high-end residential, commercial, and hospitality projects. His precision-driven methodology ensures that every technical nuance aligns perfectly with the studio's poetic vision.
