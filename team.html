Amit Das

FOUNDING PARTNER

Amit is the Founding Partner of Kala Design Co. He studied architecture at the Faculty of Architecture & Ekistics, Jamia Millia Islamia. A heritage enthusiast and dedicated designer, Amit is committed to spaces that address the sacred relationship between man and environment, responding to local economics, sociology, and history.

Kanishk Mittal

PRINCIPAL ARCHITECT & CO-FOUNDER

Kanishk is the Principal Architect and Co-founder at Kala Design Co. An alumnus of Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur, he brings extensive experience in architectural design, project management, and interior design across high-end residential, commercial, and hospitality projects.
