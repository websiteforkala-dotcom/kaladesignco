At Kala Design Co, we believe that design is a holistic discipline. It is not segmented into isolated tasks but is a fluid, continuous process of shaping environments. Our services are designed to envelop every aspect of this process, ensuring a cohesive and immersive result.

01. Interior Architecture

We begin at the structural level. Our approach to interior architecture involves defining the very bones of the space. We manipulate volume, light, and flow to create foundations that are as functional as they are beautiful. This includes spatial planning, structural modifications, and the integration of architectural details that set the tone for the entire project.

FIG 1.1 SPATIAL PLANNING PROJECT: NAKRA RESIDENCE

02. Residential Design

The home is a personal sanctuary. Our residential design service is deeply collaborative, focused on translating individual lifestyles into physical form. We curate palettes, textures, and layouts that reflect the inhabitants' unique narratives, creating spaces that offer comfort, warmth, and enduring style.

FIG 2. LIVING SPACES PROJECT: SHREE RESIDENCE

03. Commercial Environments

For brands and businesses, space is a physical manifestation of identity. We design commercial environments—from boutiques to offices—that communicate values and enhance user experience. Our designs are strategic, optimizing flow and functionality while creating a memorable atmosphere that resonates with clients and customers alike.

FIG 3. RETAIL EXPERIENCE PROJECT: ATCG STORE

04. Art Direction & Curation

A space is defined by what it holds. Our art direction service creates the visual language of a project. We source and commission art, objects, and styling elements that add layers of meaning and history. It is the final layer of storytelling that transforms a furnished room into a lived-in masterpiece.

FIG 4. CURATED OBJECTS PROJECT: MRIDUL VILLA

05. Bespoke Furniture

When the market cannot provide the perfect piece, we create it. Our bespoke furniture design produces custom tables, seating, and cabinetry tailored to the exact dimensions and aesthetic needs of a space. Designed by us and crafted by master artisans, these pieces are future heirlooms.
