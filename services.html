At Kala Design Co, we believe that design is a holistic discipline. It is not segmented into isolated tasks but is a fluid, continuous process of shaping environments. Our services are designed to envelop every aspect of this process, ensuring a cohesive and immersive result.

01. Residential Architecture & Interior

We craft personal sanctuaries that reflect your unique lifestyle and aesthetic. From expansive luxury estates to intimate urban dwellings, our team balances structural innovation with soulful interiors to create homes that are as comfortable as they are breathtaking.

FIG 1.1 PERSONAL SANCTUARY PROJECT: NAKRA RESIDENCE

02. Commercial Architecture & Interior

Transform your business identity into a physical landmark. We design high-performance workspaces, retail hubs, and hospitality venues that optimize flow, foster productivity, and leave a lasting impression on every client and employee who walks through the door.

FIG 2. BUSINESS LANDMARK PROJECT: ATCG CORPORATE

03. Factory Design

Industrial efficiency meets architectural precision. Our factory designs prioritize streamlined workflows, safety compliance, and future-ready scalability, ensuring that your production environment is a robust engine for business growth and operational excellence.

FIG 3. INDUSTRIAL PRECISION PROJECT: GANPATI ROLLINGS

04. Educational Architecture & Interior

We design vibrant learning environments that inspire curiosity and collaboration. By focusing on natural light, acoustics, and flexible layouts, we create schools and institutions that support modern pedagogy and empower both students and educators.

05. Facade Design

The face of a building is its first handshake with the world. We specialize in high-performance building envelopes that blend striking visual identity with thermal efficiency and structural integrity, giving every project a distinctive and durable exterior.

Sometimes the perfect piece doesn’t exist yet—so we build it. Our bespoke furniture design service bridges the gap between art and utility, creating custom, ergonomic, and high-quality pieces that fit perfectly within the context of your specific space.
