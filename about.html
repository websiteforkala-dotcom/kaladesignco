We Craft Spaces That Feel Inevitable, Timeless, and Profoundly Human.

A deep dive into the philosophy of restraint, texture, and silence.
FIG 1. THE MAIN OFFICE PHOTO: KALA PRESS

Founded in 2018, Kala Design Co was born from a simple yet radical idea: that interior design is not merely about decoration, but about narration. We believe that every space holds a latent story waiting to be told, a narrative that weaves together functionality, history, and artistic vision.

Our Studio, located in the heart of London's creative district, serves as a laboratory for these ideas. Here, we strip away the unnecessary to reveal the essential. In an era of noise and visual clutter, we champion the quiet power of restraint. Our team of architects and designers work in unison to create environments that do not scream for attention, but rather, command a serene presence.

We honor materials in their truest forms. Stone should look like stone; wood like wood. We reject the fake and the fleeting in favor of the authentic and the enduring. This philosophy of "Truth to Materials" guides every decision we make, from the structural layout of a penthouse to the tactile selection of a linen sofa.

But beyond the aesthetics, our work is deeply human. We design for the way people live, love, and work. A home must be a sanctuary; an office, a catalyst for thought. By understanding the rhythms of daily life, we craft spaces that support and elevate the human experience, making the ordinary feel extraordinary.

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."

As we look to the future, our mission remains clear: to build spaces that not only stand the test of time but also improve with age. Like a good pair of denim or a leather chair, we believe architecture should develop patterns of use, telling the story of those who inhabit it.

Core Principles

01. Authenticity

We honor context. Nothing is fake; everything has a purpose. We believe in the honesty of construction.

02. Simplicity

Complexity is easy; simplicity is hard. We relentlessly edit our work until only the essential remains.

03. Longevity

We don't chase trends. We design for the future, creating spaces that will age gracefully.
