Nakra Residence: A Sanctuary of Warmth and Modern Minimalism.

A contemporary interior design project located in the heart of New Delhi.
FIG 1. LIVING AREA OVERVIEW PHOTO: KALA PRESS

The Nakra Residence stands as a testament to modern luxury living, where minimalist design meets the innate warmth of natural materials. Every element has been carefully curated to create a space that feels both sophisticated and deeply inviting. Located in the bustling urban landscape of New Delhi, this home serves as a quiet retreat for its inhabitants.

Working closely with the client family, we developed a design language that reflects their appreciation for contemporary aesthetics while maintaining the comfort and functionality essential for a family home. The result is a harmonious blend of clean lines, textured surfaces, and carefully considered lighting that transforms the space throughout the day.

FIG 2. DINING & CIRCULATION PHOTO: KALA PRESS

Materiality & Texture

We chose a palette grounded in earth tones. Premium Italian marble provides a cool, sleek foundation, contrasting beautifully with the warmth of walnut wood paneling that wraps the central core of the home. Hand-tufted rugs and linen drapery add softness, absorbing sound and softening the architectural rigidity.

FIG 3. MASTER SUITE PHOTO: KALA PRESS

The lighting strategy was pivotal. Rather than relying solely on overhead downlights, we layered ambient, task, and accent lighting. Custom-fabricated brass fixtures act as jewelry for the home, adding a touch of metallic brilliance to the muted color scheme.
